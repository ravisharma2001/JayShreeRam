Ram Ji

Lord Rama was the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu who killed the demon king Ravana and restored dharma on the earth. Rama was the son of Dashrath, king of Ayodhya. His mother’s name was Kausalya. Rama is also known as “Maryada Purshottam.” He was an ideal king of all time. Rama was very handsome and his skin had a bluish shade.
